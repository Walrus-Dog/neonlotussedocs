Greetings and welcome to the Neon Lotus Somewhere Else documentation webpage. This will serve as our hub for information and updates for our project. Please take the time to look around at what is available!

Somewhere Else will be our first game. A 3d first person parkour puzzle exploration game set in an abandoned city with an aura of unease. I have made this new site in order to gather my most recent thoughts on this project more easily and have a better structure and flow to our concepts.

Content will be added to this site as it comes to mind and is fleshed out more. Things are subject to change. If you have any questions about anything make sure to ask me.
